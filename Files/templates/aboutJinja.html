About & Your Privacy

Who are We?

Welcome to Horizon Travels, your go-to destination for booking flights. At Horizon Travels, we strive to make air travel easy and hassle-free for our customers. Our team of travel experts is dedicated to providing you with the best flight deals and personalized service to ensure that you have a smooth and enjoyable travel experience.

As a leading flight booking website, we offer a wide selection of flights from various airlines, allowing you to choose the one that best fits your travel needs and budget. We understand that air travel can be stressful, which is why we provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision, from flight schedules and routes to baggage allowances and airline policies.

At Horizon Travels, we believe in putting our customers first. Our user-friendly platform is designed to make the flight booking process quick and easy, while our dedicated customer support team is available around the clock to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

Thank you for choosing Horizon Travels for your flight booking needs. We look forward to helping you discover the world, one flight at a time.

What Data Do We Collect?

As a booking website, we collect both user data and booking data to provide our services.

How Will Your Data Be Used?

User data such as email addresses and names are necessary to create accounts and to communicate with users regarding their bookings. We also collect booking data such as travel dates, destinations, and the number of travelers to facilitate the booking process. We value the privacy of our users and do not share their data with any third parties. At our website, we aim to ensure that all user data is kept secure and only used for the purposes of providing our booking services.

The Security of Your Data

At our website, we take the security of user data very seriously. All user passwords are hashed using a strong one-way hashing algorithm, which means that even if our database were compromised, user passwords would not be easily recoverable. In addition, all data stored on our servers is encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption algorithms. This ensures that even if someone were able to access our servers, they would not be able to read the data without the decryption key. We regularly monitor our systems and employ various security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or data breaches. At our website, you can be confident that your data is secure and protected.
